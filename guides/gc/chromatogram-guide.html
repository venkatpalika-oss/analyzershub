Chromatogram Interpretation Guide

Tip: Always stabilize sampling and carrier first. Most GC problems are sampling problems — not detector problems.

Quick Checks

No peaks: Sample blocked, valve not switching, carrier pressure low.

Retention time shift: Oven temperature unstable, carrier pressure hunting.

Noisy baseline: Contamination, detector issue, grounding or shielding problem.

Repeatability Rule

After maintenance, confirm 2–3 stable consecutive runs before releasing analyzer results to operations.
