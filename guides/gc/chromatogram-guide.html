Chromatogram Interpretation Guide

Tip: Always stabilize sampling + carrier first. GC problems are often sampling problems.

Quick Checks

No peaks: sample blocked / valve not switching / carrier low.

Retention shift: oven temp unstable / carrier pressure hunting.

Noisy baseline: contamination / detector issue / grounding.

Repeatability Rule

After maintenance, confirm 2–3 stable consecutive runs before releasing results.
