CEMS Audit Failure

When a “healthy analyzer” still fails regulatory inspection.

Incident Summary

During a statutory audit, a stack-mounted CEMS was declared non-compliant despite stable readings and no active alarms.

Observed Symptoms

  • Analyzer values stable but rejected
  • Zero/span drift beyond limits
  • Data flagged during audit review

Root Cause

Calibration gas traceability and response time were not compliant with QAL requirements.

What Went Wrong

  • Expired calibration certificates
  • No response-time documentation
  • Irregular zero/span frequency

Corrective Action

  • Revalidated calibration gases
  • Documented response time
  • Implemented audit checklist
Lesson: CEMS audits fail due to documentation and discipline, not analyzer hardware.
