GC Carrier Gas Contamination

Stable pressure — unstable chromatograms.

Symptoms

  • Retention time drift
  • Baseline noise
  • Poor repeatability

Root Cause

Moisture and oil contamination in carrier gas due to missing purifier maintenance.

Diagnosis Mistake

Columns and detectors were replaced before checking gas quality.

Fix

  • Replaced purifier cartridges
  • Installed moisture indicator
  • Validated carrier purity
Rule: If chromatograms drift — check carrier gas before touching columns.
