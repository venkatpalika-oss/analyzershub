Servomex 4900 — Alarm Decision Trees

Technician-first alarm handling using decision-tree logic. Follow the sequence strictly to avoid unnecessary calibration or sensor replacement.

← Back to Analyzer Servomex Analyzers →
Golden rule: Always stabilize sample flow & pressure before acting on alarms.

Sample Flow Alarm

Insufficient or unstable sample flow.

Do not calibrate with unstable flow.

Oxygen Sensor Fault

Paramagnetic O₂ signal outside limits.

Sensor replacement is the last step, not the first.

Communication Alarm

Analyzer data not updating in DCS.

Many comm alarms are DCS quality-bit issues, not analyzer faults.
