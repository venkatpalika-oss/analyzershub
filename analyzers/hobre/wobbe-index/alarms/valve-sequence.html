Decision Tree: Sampling / Conditioning

← Back to Alarms Hub Alarm Codes → Back to Analyzer
Goal: clean, dry, stable sample. Most Wobbe errors come from sample conditioning.
1) Check filters/coalescers. Replace if contaminated or blocked.
2) Verify drains: ensure no liquid accumulation and drains are functioning.
3) Check for moisture/dewpoint issues; confirm heat tracing (if used).
4) Check for liquid carryover during process upsets (major cause of spikes).
5) After correction: verify stable trend, then compare to reference.

Major Troubleshooting

  • Spikes/jumps: liquid carryover, drains blocked, sudden composition change.
  • Slow drift: moisture, contamination, filter saturation.
  • Calibration fails: sample not stable or calibration gas flow incorrect.
