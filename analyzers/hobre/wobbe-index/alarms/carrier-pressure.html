Decision Tree: Pressure / Flow

← Back to Alarms Hub Alarm Codes → Back to Analyzer
Goal: stable pressure/flow (no hunting). Unstable flow = unstable Wobbe output.
1) Verify upstream supply pressure and isolation valves are open.
2) Check regulator hunting/oscillation. If hunting, repair/replace regulator.
3) Leak test fittings and tubing (soap test or approved method). Repair and retest.
4) Check restrictions: clogged filters, blocked tubing, stuck needle valve/restrictor.
5) Confirm stable trend for sufficient time (not one reading).

Major Troubleshooting

  • Wobbe jumps with pressure: regulator hunting / unstable supply.
  • Wobbe low: restriction, low supply, leakage, filter blockage.
  • After fixing pressure: do NOT calibrate immediately—stabilize first.

Calibration Note

Calibrate only after pressure/flow is stable and no sample conditioning faults are active.
