Temperature / Warm-up Alarm (Portable NGC)

1) Warm-up complete?
Allow full warm-up before expecting stable retention and values.
Cold ambient conditions increase warm-up time.
2) Power stability
Low battery/unstable AC can trigger heater/temperature faults.
3) Ventilation
Ensure vents are not blocked during transport/use.
Verify: temperature holds steady for 10–15 minutes, then run test.
