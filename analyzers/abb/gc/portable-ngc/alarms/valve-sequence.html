Valve / Sequence Alarm (Portable NGC)

1) After transport checks
Transport vibration can loosen connections and affect switching/injection.
2) Observe switching
Confirm commanded steps occur; rerun a cycle after restart if allowed.
3) Sample/carryover
Contamination or liquid carryover can mimic valve faults.
Verify: successful run + stable repeatability on 2–3 cycles.
