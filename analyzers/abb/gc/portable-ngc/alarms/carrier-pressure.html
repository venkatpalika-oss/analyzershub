Decision Tree: Carrier / Pressure / Flow

← Back to Alarms Hub ABB GC Codes → Chromatogram Guide
Goal: Stabilize carrier pressure and remove leaks before running field results.
1) Confirm carrier source and valve are open.
2) Verify regulator stability (no hunting).
3) Leak test all quick-connects and fittings.
4) Confirm inlet pressure is stable.
5) Run 2–3 cycles and verify repeatability.
