Portable note: Many “GC issues” are actually setup issues — wrong sample pressure, loose fittings, unstable power, or unstable carrier.

Quick Setup Checklist (Field)

1) Stable power (battery/AC) and warm-up completed
2) Sample connection tight + leak tested
3) Sample pressure within recommended range (stable)
4) Carrier supply stable (no hunting)
Verification: run 2–3 cycles and confirm repeatability.
