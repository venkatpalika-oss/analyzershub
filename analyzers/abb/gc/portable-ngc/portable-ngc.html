Portable tip: Most issues are from setup: leaks, unstable pressure, dirty regulators, or moisture carryover.

Field Setup Checklist

1) Leak test sample and carrier connections
2) Stabilize pressures before starting runs
3) Use clean filters and moisture control
4) Run 2–3 repeatable cycles before reporting
