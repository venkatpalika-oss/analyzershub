Valve / Sequence Alarm (PGC5007B)

1) Instrument air
Verify pressure and quality (dry, oil-free). Wet/dirty air causes sticking.
#1 cause: water/oil in air → valves stick → injection fails.
2) Observe switching
Confirm solenoids energize as expected and valves physically switch.
3) Solenoid check
Check coil/wiring/indication. Swap with spare (if available) to confirm fault.
4) Mechanical contamination
If sticking persists, valve block may be contaminated → service/replace.
Verify: run completes + chromatogram returns normal.
