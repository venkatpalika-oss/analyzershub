Decision Tree: Valve / Sequence

← Back to Alarms Hub ABB GC Codes → Chromatogram Guide
Goal: Ensure valve switching and sequence timing are correct.
1) Verify instrument air quality and pressure.
2) Check solenoid actuation and associated wiring.
3) If peaks are wrong or missing: suspect sticking valves, leakage, or contamination.
4) Confirm method sequence timing.
5) Run 2–3 cycles and verify repeatability.
