ABB PGC5007B — Total Sulfur Analysis

Troubleshooting reference for total sulfur configurations: carrier, sampling conditioning, oven stability, valves, comms.

Alarm Decision Trees → ABB GC Alarm Codes → Chromatogram Guide → ← Back to Search
Special note (Sulfur): sampling cleanliness + conditioning is critical. Liquids/contamination can cause unstable results.

Recommended Flow

1) Check alarms + last stable sulfur reading time
2) Stabilize carrier pressure and verify no leaks
3) Verify sample conditioning (filters/traps/heaters if used)
4) Confirm oven stability
5) Verify valve sequencing and comms
