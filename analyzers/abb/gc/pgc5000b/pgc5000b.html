ABB PGC5000B — Smart Oven Series

Process GC troubleshooting: sampling, carrier stability, oven control, valves, and communications.

Alarm Decision Trees → ABB GC Alarm Codes → Chromatogram Guide → Back to Search
Safety: follow site procedures for hazardous gases. Isolate, depressurize, and leak test after work.

Recommended Troubleshooting Flow

1) Check alarms + time of last good run
2) Verify carrier pressure stability (no hunting)
3) Verify sample conditioning (dry, clean filters)
4) Confirm oven temperature stable at setpoint
5) Verify valves switch; instrument air clean/dry
