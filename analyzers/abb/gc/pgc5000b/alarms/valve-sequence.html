Decision Tree: Valve / Sequence

← Back to Alarms Hub ABB GC Codes → Chromatogram Guide
Goal: confirm valves switch correctly and method timing is consistent.
1) Check instrument air supply: pressure OK, clean/dry, no water/oil.
2) Verify solenoid actuation (click/indicator). Check power/wiring if dead.
3) If valve clicks but results wrong: suspect sticking/leakage/contamination.
4) Confirm method sequence timing vs last known good configuration.
5) Run 2–3 cycles and verify repeatable peaks.
