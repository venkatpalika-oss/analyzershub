Decision Tree: Carrier / Pressure / Flow

← Back to Alarms Hub ABB GC Codes → Chromatogram Guide
Goal: stabilize carrier pressure (no hunting) and remove leaks before changing method settings.
1) Confirm carrier cylinder pressure is adequate and isolation valves are open.
2) Check regulator stability (no oscillation). Replace/repair if hunting.
3) Leak test carrier line connections and manifold fittings.
4) Verify carrier inlet pressure at analyzer is within spec and steady.
5) Run 2–3 cycles and confirm peaks return + repeatability improves.
