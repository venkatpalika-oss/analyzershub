Carrier / Pressure / Flow Alarm (PGC5000B)

1) Confirm carrier supply
Cylinder/bundle pressure OK, correct carrier type, main valve open.
If empty / wrong carrier → no peaks / run abort.
2) Regulator stability
Observe outlet pressure: no hunting, no creep, stable during analysis.
3) Leak check (fast)
Leak test fittings from regulator to manifold and critical junctions.
Micro-leaks cause retention shifts and repeatability issues.
4) Restrictor / filter blockage
Check for plugged filters/orifice restricting flow.
Verify: 2–3 stable runs, retention/area repeatable.
