Decision Tree: Valve / Sequence

← Back to Alarms Hub ABB GC Codes → Chromatogram Guide
Goal: Confirm valves switch correctly and method timing is consistent.
1) Check instrument air supply (clean, dry) and pressure stability.
2) Verify solenoid actuation (click or indicator). Check wiring or power if dead.
3) If valve moves but results are wrong: suspect sticking, leakage, or contamination.
4) Verify method sequence timing against the last known good configuration.
5) Run 2–3 cycles and confirm repeatable peaks.
