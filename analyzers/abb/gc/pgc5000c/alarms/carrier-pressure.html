Decision Tree: Carrier / Pressure / Flow

← Back to Alarms Hub ABB GC Codes → Chromatogram Guide
Goal: stabilize carrier pressure (no hunting) and remove leaks before touching method settings.
1) Confirm carrier supply valve open and cylinder pressure adequate.
2) Check regulator stability (no oscillation). Replace/repair if hunting.
3) Leak test carrier fittings/manifold with approved leak method.
4) Verify inlet pressure at analyzer is within spec and steady.
5) Run 2–3 cycles and confirm peaks return + repeatability improves.
