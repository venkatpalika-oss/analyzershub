Decision Tree: Carrier / Pressure / Flow

← Back to Alarms Hub ABB GC Codes → Chromatogram Guide
Goal: Stabilize carrier pressure (no hunting) and remove leaks before touching method settings.
1) Confirm carrier supply valve is open and cylinder pressure is adequate.
2) Check regulator stability (no oscillation). Repair or replace if hunting.
3) Leak test carrier fittings and manifold using an approved leak detection method.
4) Verify inlet pressure at the analyzer is within specification and steady.
5) Run 2–3 cycles and confirm peaks return and repeatability improves.
