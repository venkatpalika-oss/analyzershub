ABB AZ25 – Alarm Decision Trees

Alarm troubleshooting guidance for ABB AZ25 TDLAS analyzers. These alarms are commonly related to optical path, signal strength, alignment, and process conditions.

Technician note: AZ25 alarms are often caused by beam attenuation, window fouling, purge issues, or process disturbances. Always verify optical path condition before replacing hardware.

Common ABB AZ25 Alarms

  • No Signal / Beam Lost
  • Low Signal Strength / Attenuation
  • Calibration Failure
  • Purge / Reference Path Fault
