ABB AZ40 – Alarm Decision Trees

Alarm troubleshooting guidance for ABB AZ40 TDLAS-based gas analyzers used in demanding and high-temperature process conditions.

Technician note: AZ40 alarms are most often caused by optical window fouling, beam misalignment, purge flow loss, or severe process disturbances. Always verify optical path condition before replacing electronics.

Common ABB AZ40 Alarms

  • No Signal / Beam Lost
  • Low Signal / Attenuation
  • Calibration Fault
  • Purge / Reference Path Fault
