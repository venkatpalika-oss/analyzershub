Agilent Process Gas Chromatograph

Online gas chromatographs for continuous monitoring of process stream composition.

Back to Analyzer Search →

Overview

Agilent process GCs are used in refineries and petrochemical plants for real-time analysis of gas and liquid streams. Reliability depends heavily on sample conditioning, carrier gas stability, and valve sequencing.

Working Principle

Gas components are separated in chromatographic columns and detected using thermal conductivity or other detectors.

Main Components

  • Sample conditioning system
  • Carrier gas system
  • Injection valves and loops
  • Oven and column modules
  • Detector and electronics

Calibration

Calibration is performed using certified standard gases. Stable pressures and repeatable valve timing are critical.

Typical Field Issues

  • Poor repeatability due to pressure instability
  • Retention time shift
  • Valve wear or sticking
  • Moisture contamination
