AMETEK 888 — Trend & Response Time Guide

Understanding analyzer dynamics helps differentiate real SRU process upsets from analyzer issues.

Typical Response Characteristics

  • Initial response: 30–60 seconds
  • 90% response time (T90): 2–5 minutes
  • Full stabilization: 5–10 minutes
Slower response usually indicates sample line fouling, sulfur condensation, or heater performance issues.

Normal Trend Behavior

  • Smooth trend following SRU load changes
  • No oscillation during steady operation
  • Stable baseline after calibration

Abnormal Trend Patterns

  • Flat line → sample blockage or probe plugging
  • Slow ramp → sulfur deposition in sample system
  • Noisy signal → grounding or detector contamination
  • Sudden step change → actual process upset
