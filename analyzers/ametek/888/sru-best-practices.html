SRU Analyzer Best Practices

Proven field practices for tail gas analyzers in sulfur recovery units.

Sample System

  • Maintain sample temperature above sulfur dew point
  • Minimize dead volume and cold spots
  • Use corrosion-resistant materials

Calibration Strategy

  • Use certified sulfur calibration gases
  • Perform calibration during stable SRU operation
  • Trend before and after calibration

Operational Tips

  • Do not ignore slow drift — it indicates contamination
  • Investigate analyzer alarms immediately
  • Correlate analyzer trends with SRU process data
Analyzer problems in SRU are usually sample-system related, not analyzer electronics failures.
