AMETEK 933 — Trends & Response Guide

Understanding normal trend behavior helps distinguish analyzer issues from real process changes.

Typical Response Characteristics

  • Initial response: 10–30 seconds
  • 90% response time (T90): 1–3 minutes
  • Full stabilization: 3–5 minutes
Slower response usually indicates sample flow restriction or sensor aging.

Normal Trend Patterns

  • Smooth response to load or air changes
  • Stable baseline after calibration
  • No oscillation during steady operation

Abnormal Trend Patterns

  • Flat line → blocked sample or dead sensor
  • Noisy trend → moisture or electrical interference
  • Slow drift → air leakage or sensor aging
  • Sudden spike → process upset or leak introduction
