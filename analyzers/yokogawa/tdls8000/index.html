Yokogawa TDLS8000 Gas Analyzer

Analyzer Overview

The Yokogawa TDLS8000 is a Tunable Diode Laser Spectroscopy (TDLS) analyzer for in-situ gas measurement. It provides fast, accurate, and continuous monitoring of specific gases with minimal maintenance.

Key Features

  • Laser-based high-speed gas measurement
  • Non-contact in-situ measurement
  • Stable performance with low maintenance
  • Multiple gas measurement capability

Typical Applications

  • Combustion gas monitoring
  • Emissions and process gas analysis
  • Boilers, furnaces, and kilns
  • Environmental monitoring

Maintenance & Calibration Notes

  • Check laser output and optical alignment
  • Ensure measurement path is free from dust/smoke
  • Perform calibration with correct gas standard
  • Inspect electronics module periodically
