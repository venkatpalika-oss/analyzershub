pH Analyzers

pH analyzers measure the acidity or alkalinity of liquids using electrochemical sensors. In real plants, reliability depends far more on sensor condition, sample quality, and installation than on the transmitter itself.

Field reality: If pH is unstable, drifting, or slow, always inspect the sensor first before suspecting the analyzer electronics.
