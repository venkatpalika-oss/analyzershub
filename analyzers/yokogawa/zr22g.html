Yokogawa ZR22G Oxygen Analyzer

Manufacturer: Yokogawa

Analyzer Type: In-situ Zirconia Oxygen Analyzer

1. Overview

The Yokogawa ZR22G is an in-situ zirconia oxygen analyzer designed for continuous oxygen measurement in combustion processes such as boilers, furnaces, heaters, and incinerators.

  • Zirconia sensor probe

4. Installation Notes

  • Install probe at correct insertion length as per flue diameter
  • Avoid locations with excessive vibration
  • Ensure clean and dry reference air supply
  • Allow sufficient straight duct length upstream

5. Common Alarms & Probable Causes

7. Calibration Guidelines

  • Ensure analyzer has completed warm-up
  • Use certified calibration gases
  • Verify calibration valve operation
  • Allow sufficient stabilization time

8. Preventive Maintenance

  • Inspect reference air filter monthly
  • Check heater current during routine maintenance
  • Perform calibration as per site SOP
  • Inspect probe for dust or ash buildup

9. Field Tips

  • Never judge sensor health before full warm-up
  • Reference air problems cause most false readings
  • Sudden drift often indicates sensor contamination
  • Always check heater status first during faults
