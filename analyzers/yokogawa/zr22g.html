Yokogawa ZR22G Zirconia Oxygen Analyzer

Analyzer Overview

The Yokogawa ZR22G is a high-performance zirconia oxygen analyzer used for continuous oxygen measurement in combustion processes such as boilers, furnaces, kilns, and incinerators.

Key Features

  • High accuracy zirconia sensor technology
  • Fast response time
  • Suitable for high-temperature flue gas applications
  • Long sensor life with proper maintenance

Typical Applications

  • Boilers and furnaces
  • Cement kilns
  • Incinerators
  • Power plant combustion control
