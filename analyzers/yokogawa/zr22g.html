Yokogawa ZR22G Oxygen Analyzer

Overview

Zirconia-based in-situ oxygen analyzer used in boilers, furnaces, and process heaters.

Measurement Principle

Uses zirconium oxide sensor with reference air and heater control.

Common Problems

  • No oxygen reading
  • Heater alarm
  • High drift

Troubleshooting – No Signal

  1. Check heater resistance
  2. Verify reference air flow
  3. Check sensor aging
