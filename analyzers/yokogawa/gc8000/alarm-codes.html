Yokogawa GC8000 – Alarm Codes

Common GC8000 alarms, symptoms, and recommended first actions.

← Back to GC8000 Alarm Decision Trees → Back to Search

Typical GC8000 Alarm Categories

  • Carrier Pressure Low / High – check supply, regulator stability, leaks
  • Oven Not Ready – temperature not reaching setpoint
  • Injection / Valve Error – valve switching or timing issue
  • Detector Fault – electronics, noise, or instability
  • Communication Error – Modbus/OPC mapping or network problem

Field Best Practice

  • Always resolve carrier & sampling alarms before touching electronics
  • Verify with 2–3 stable runs after clearing alarms
  • GC alarms often indicate process or utility issues, not GC failure
