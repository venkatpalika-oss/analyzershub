Carrier / Pressure / Flow Alarm

Step 1 – Carrier Gas Supply
Verify cylinder pressure, correct gas type, main valve fully open.
Empty or wrong carrier gas → No peaks / injection failure.
Step 2 – Regulator Stability
Check outlet pressure for hunting or drop during run.
Step 3 – Leak Check
Leak test regulator, tubing, valve manifold, detector fittings.
Pressure stable → Peaks return → Alarm clears after successful run.
