Decision Tree: Oven / Temperature

← Back to Alarms Hub Alarm Codes → Chromatogram Guide
Goal: stabilize oven temperature at setpoint (no oscillation) before judging repeatability or peak shifts.
1) Confirm the alarm type: “Oven Not Ready”, “Heater Fault”, or “Temp Deviation”. Note timestamp.
2) Compare Setpoint vs Actual. If Actual is far below setpoint, suspect heater/power path.
3) Check for airflow/ventilation issues: blocked vents, fan failure, cabinet overheating.
4) If temperature hunts (up/down): check sensor integrity, loose connections, controller tuning (if accessible).
5) After fixing: wait for stabilization, then run 2–3 cycles and confirm stable retention time.

Quick Symptoms → Likely Cause

Oven never reaches setpoint: heater/power, relay, blown fuse, or airflow too high.

Oven overshoots and hunts: sensor issue, loose wiring, tuning/control instability.

Retention time shift: temperature instability or carrier pressure hunting (check both).
