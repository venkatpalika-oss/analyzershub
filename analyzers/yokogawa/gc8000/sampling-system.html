Yokogawa GC8000 – Sampling System

Correct sampling is critical — most GC problems originate here.

Typical GC8000 Sampling Components

  • Sample probe / extraction point
  • Fast loop & flow control
  • Filters / coalescers
  • Pressure regulators
  • Sample return or vent

Common Sampling Problems

Liquid carryover: causes unstable baseline and poor repeatability
Unstable pressure: leads to retention time shifts
Plugged filters: cause slow response or no peaks

Recommended Sampling Checklist

1) Verify sample pressure is stable and within design limits
2) Drain traps and inspect filters
3) Leak test fittings and fast loop
4) Confirm return / vent line is not blocked
