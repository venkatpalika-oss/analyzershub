Yokogawa GC8000

A high-speed process gas chromatograph designed for reliable and accurate online gas analysis in demanding industrial environments.

Overview

The Yokogawa GC8000 is a process gas chromatograph optimized for fast cycle times and stable long-term operation. It is widely used for continuous monitoring of gas composition in refineries, petrochemical plants, and gas processing facilities.

Typical Applications

Refineries

Hydrocarbon stream analysis, hydrogen purity monitoring, and fuel gas control.

Petrochemical Plants

Feedstock and product composition analysis for process optimization.

Natural Gas Processing

Continuous gas composition analysis for blending and quality monitoring.

Operation & Troubleshooting

Startup Checks

  • Confirm carrier gas pressure and quality
  • Verify oven temperature stability
  • Ensure detector baseline is stable

Common Operational Issues

  • Baseline instability due to moisture or contamination
  • Poor peak separation from incorrect oven temperature
  • Unstable detector signals caused by leaks

Calibration Issues

  • Incorrect calibration gas selection
  • Valve timing mismatch
  • Detector sensitivity drift

Maintenance Guidelines

Columns

Inspect column efficiency and replace when peak broadening is observed.

Detectors

Check detector cleanliness and signal stability during routine maintenance.

Valves & Sample System

Verify valve timing, sample flow rates, and sample conditioning performance.

Integration & Communication

GC8000 supports seamless integration with Yokogawa DCS and third-party control systems, enabling real-time data exchange for advanced process control.
