Recommended Troubleshooting Flow

1. Check active alarms and timestamps
2. Verify carrier pressure and sample pressure stability
3. Confirm oven temperature holds steady
4. Inspect sample conditioning (filters, traps, drains)
5. Review chromatogram and repeatability
Rule: GC results are only as good as the sampling system and carrier stability.

Preventive Maintenance (Quick)
