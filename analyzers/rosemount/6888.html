Rosemount 6888 — Zirconia Oxygen Analyzer

High-temperature zirconia oxygen analyzer used in CEMS, boilers, furnaces, and combustion control applications.

Analyzer Overview

The Rosemount 6888 is a zirconia-based oxygen analyzer designed for continuous measurement of oxygen in flue gas and combustion processes. It is widely used in power plants, refineries, and industrial furnaces where high-temperature operation and fast response are required.

Key Features

  • Zirconia oxygen sensor with integrated heater
  • Fast response time and high measurement accuracy
  • Designed for harsh, high-temperature environments
  • Supports field calibration and onboard diagnostics

Typical Applications

  • Boilers and fired heaters
  • Continuous Emissions Monitoring Systems (CEMS)
  • Combustion control and efficiency optimization
  • Industrial furnaces and incinerators

Maintenance & Calibration Notes

  • Allow heater temperature to fully stabilize before trusting readings
  • Use certified zero and span gases for calibration
  • Inspect wiring, heater circuits, and electronics periodically
  • Check zirconia sensor for contamination, cracking, or aging
Frequent calibration failure or drift usually indicates sensor aging, heater problems, or sample process issues.
