Siemens MAXUM Edition II – Alarm Codes

Common alarm categories, symptoms, and recommended first actions (technician quick reference).

← Back to MAXUM II Alarm Decision Trees → Back to Search

Typical MAXUM II Alarm Categories

  • Carrier Pressure / Flow – supply low, regulator hunting, leaks, restriction
  • Oven Not Ready / Temperature Deviation – heater, sensor, airflow/venting
  • Valve / Sequence / Injection – solenoid/air issues, sticking valves, timing mismatch
  • Detector / Electronics – noise, instability, signal out of range (check grounding & contamination first)
  • Communication / I/O – network, Modbus/OPC settings, mapping/scaling, quality status

Field Best Practice

  • Resolve carrier & sampling issues before touching method/electronics.
  • After corrective action, verify with 2–3 stable runs.
  • Many “GC faults” are utility (air/power) or sampling problems.
