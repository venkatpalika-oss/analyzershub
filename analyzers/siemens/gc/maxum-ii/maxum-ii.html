Siemens MAXUM Edition II

A flexible and reliable process gas chromatograph designed for continuous online analysis in refineries, petrochemical plants, and gas processing facilities.

Overview

The Siemens MAXUM Edition II is a modular process gas chromatograph capable of handling multiple analytical streams with high accuracy and long-term stability. It is widely used for real-time composition analysis, quality monitoring, and process control.

Typical Applications

Refineries

Hydrogen purity, refinery gas analysis, and hydrocarbon composition monitoring.

Petrochemical Plants

Feedstock analysis, product quality assurance, and process optimization.

Natural Gas Processing

Composition analysis for custody transfer, blending, and heating value calculation.

Operation & Troubleshooting

Startup Checks

  • Verify carrier gas pressure and purity
  • Confirm oven temperature stability
  • Check detector readiness and signal baseline

Common Operational Issues

  • Baseline drift due to contamination or column aging
  • Poor peak resolution caused by incorrect oven temperature
  • Unstable detector signal from carrier gas leaks

Calibration Problems

  • Incorrect calibration gas composition
  • Valve timing mismatch
  • Detector sensitivity drift

Maintenance Guidelines

Columns

Monitor column efficiency and replace if peak broadening or retention shifts occur.

Detectors

Inspect detector cleanliness, signal stability, and response factors periodically.

Valves & Sample System

Check valve timing, sample flow rates, and ensure sample conditioning is adequate.

Integration & Communication

MAXUM Edition II integrates seamlessly with DCS and PLC systems via standard industrial communication protocols, providing real-time analytical data for process control and optimization.
