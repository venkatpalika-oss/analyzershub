Siemens SITRANS CV

A dedicated process gas chromatograph for continuous measurement of calorific value, Wobbe index, and natural gas composition in custody transfer and energy applications.

Overview

Siemens SITRANS CV is designed specifically for natural gas quality measurement. It delivers fast and reliable calorific value, Wobbe index, density, and gas composition data for billing, blending, and regulatory compliance.

Typical Applications

Custody Transfer

Accurate energy content measurement for commercial gas handover points.

Gas Distribution Networks

Monitoring calorific value and Wobbe index across pipelines and city gate stations.

Gas Blending

Ensuring blended gas meets contractual and regulatory specifications.

Operation & Troubleshooting

Startup Checks

  • Verify carrier gas pressure and purity
  • Confirm oven and detector temperatures are stable
  • Check sample conditioning system operation

Common Measurement Issues

  • Incorrect CV or Wobbe due to contaminated sample lines
  • Unstable readings from moisture or heavy hydrocarbons
  • Baseline drift caused by carrier gas impurities

Calibration & Validation

  • Use certified natural gas calibration mixtures
  • Verify response factors periodically
  • Validate calculated CV and Wobbe values

Maintenance Guidelines

Sample Conditioning

Inspect filters, regulators, and moisture traps to ensure clean sample delivery.

Columns

Monitor column performance and replace if separation quality degrades.

Detector & Valves

Check detector signal stability and verify valve timing regularly.

Integration & Communication

SITRANS CV integrates easily with flow computers, DCS, and SCADA systems, providing real-time energy data through standard industrial communication protocols.
