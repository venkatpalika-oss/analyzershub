Siemens SITRANS CV — Calorific Value / Wobbe Index GC

Technician troubleshooting reference for CV/Wobbe measurement: sampling, carrier stability, oven stability, valve sequencing, calculation reliability, and communication to DCS/PLC.

← Back to Search Alarm Decision Trees → Alarm Codes → Chromatogram Guide →
Important: CV/Wobbe instability is often caused by sampling changes (moisture, liquid carryover, contamination) or carrier pressure hunting. Fix utilities & sampling first.

Common SITRANS CV Symptoms

CV/Wobbe jumping: sampling quality issue, pressure hunting, inconsistent cycle time.

No update to DCS: comms/mapping/quality status issue.

Wrong CV: wrong component mapping, scaling, or calculation inputs.

Recommended Troubleshooting Order

1) Confirm analyzer is cycling (timestamps update)
2) Stabilize carrier pressure (no hunting)
3) Check sample conditioning (filters, drains, dewpoint/liquid)
4) Confirm oven temperature stable
5) Confirm valve switching + method timing
