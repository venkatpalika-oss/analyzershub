Siemens SITRANS CV – Alarm Codes

Common categories and first actions for CV/Wobbe GC measurement reliability.

← Back to SITRANS CV Alarm Decision Trees → Back to Search

Typical SITRANS CV Alarm Categories

  • Carrier Pressure / Flow – supply low, regulator hunting, leaks, restrictions
  • Oven Not Ready / Temperature Deviation – heater, sensor, airflow/venting
  • Valve / Sequence / Injection – instrument air/solenoid, sticking valves, timing mismatch
  • Calculation / CV-Wobbe Quality – unstable composition, mapping/scaling errors, invalid component list
  • Communication / DCS – network, Modbus/OPC settings, quality/status mapping

CV/Wobbe Specific Tips

  • Check for liquid carryover and moisture: it causes false composition and unstable CV.
  • Verify component mapping and scaling before blaming calculation.
  • Confirm GC cycle time matches DCS expectations; avoid too-fast polling.
