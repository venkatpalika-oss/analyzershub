Decision Tree: Carrier / Pressure / Flow

← Back to Alarms Hub Alarm Codes → Chromatogram Guide
Goal: stabilize carrier pressure (no hunting). This directly affects retention time and CV/Wobbe stability.
1) Verify carrier supply and isolation valves open (cylinder/bundle/manifold).
2) Check regulator stability. If hunting/oscillation exists, replace/repair regulator.
3) Leak test carrier lines/fittings/manifold. Repair and retest.
4) Check restrictions: filters, capillary/tubing blockage, kinked lines.
5) Run 2–3 cycles and confirm retention time + CV/Wobbe are stable.

Quick Symptoms → Likely Cause

CV/Wobbe jumping: carrier hunting, sampling liquid carryover, inconsistent cycle.

No peaks: carrier off/low, restriction, major leak.

Retention shift: carrier pressure unstable or oven unstable (check both).
