Decision Tree: Oven / Temperature

← Back to Alarms Hub Alarm Codes → Chromatogram Guide
Goal: stabilize oven temperature at setpoint. Temperature deviation creates retention drift and unstable CV/Wobbe.
1) Compare setpoint vs actual. Note deviation/not ready alarms.
2) If actual stays low: check heater power path, fuses, relays, wiring.
3) If hunting/overshoot: check sensor integrity, loose wiring, airflow/fan operation.
4) Confirm cabinet ventilation and ambient conditions are acceptable.
5) Run 2–3 cycles; confirm stable retention time and stable CV/Wobbe output.
