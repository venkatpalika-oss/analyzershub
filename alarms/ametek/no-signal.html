Alarm: No Signal / Analyzer Offline

Manufacturer: AMETEK
Applies to: CEMS, extractive, and in-situ analyzers

Analyzer output must not be used while this alarm is active.

Typical Causes

  • Loss of power or internal PSU fault
  • Analyzer CPU or communication failure
  • Sample system failure preventing measurement
  • Severe optical attenuation or blocked cell
  • I/O or network configuration fault

Field Checks

  • Verify analyzer power and internal status LEDs
  • Check analyzer HMI or controller messages
  • Confirm sample flow and pump operation
  • Inspect optical cell or measurement chamber
  • Check signal mapping to DCS / DAHS

Corrective Actions

  • Restore power or replace PSU
  • Restart analyzer controller
  • Restore sample flow or clean measurement cell
  • Repair communication or I/O configuration
