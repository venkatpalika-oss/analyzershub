Alarm: Sample Pump Failure

Indicates the analyzer sample pump is not operating or delivering flow.

Probable Causes

  • Pump motor failure
  • Electrical supply or fuse fault
  • Liquid carryover damaging pump
  • Blocked inlet or outlet

Corrective Actions

  • Replace or repair pump
  • Restore electrical supply
  • Drain moisture and service sample system
