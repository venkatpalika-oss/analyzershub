Alarm: Calibration Failure

Occurs when the analyzer does not reach expected values during zero or span calibration.

Probable Causes

  • Calibration gas not reaching analyzer
  • Incorrect or expired calibration gas
  • Calibration solenoid valve malfunction
  • Leaks or blockage in calibration tubing
  • Degraded analyzer response

Corrective Actions

  • Verify calibration gas pressure and flow
  • Check calibration valve operation
  • Replace tubing or fittings
  • Replace sensor or service analyzer if required
