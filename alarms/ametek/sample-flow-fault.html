Alarm: Sample Flow Fault

Indicates insufficient or no sample gas flow through the analyzer.

Probable Causes

  • Sample pump failure
  • Blocked filters or sample lines
  • Condensation or moisture carryover
  • Sample probe or nozzle blockage
  • Flow sensor malfunction

Corrective Actions

  • Inspect and clean sample filters
  • Check pump operation
  • Remove moisture or restore heat tracing
  • Clear probe or sample line blockage
