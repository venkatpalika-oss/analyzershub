Alarm: Optical / Measurement Cell Fault

Indicates a problem with the optical path or measurement cell.

Probable Causes

  • Dirty or contaminated optical windows
  • Severe sample gas attenuation
  • Light source or detector failure
  • Cell pressure or temperature abnormal

Corrective Actions

  • Clean optical windows
  • Restore sample conditions
  • Replace lamp or detector module
