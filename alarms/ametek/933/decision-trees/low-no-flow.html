Decision Tree: Low / No Flow

Symptom: low flow alarm, no sample flow, slow response, unstable reading.

← Back to Trees ← Alarm Codes AMETEK 933 Main →
Safety: Follow permit rules before opening sample lines. Depressurize safely.

Isolation steps

1) Confirm sample source pressure is available. Check upstream isolation valves are open.
2) Check filter condition: if DP high or bowl dirty, replace/clean filter and re-test flow.
3) Check regulator and needle valve settings: ensure not fully closed and not hunting.
4) If pump fitted:
  • Check pump power and status
  • Check pump diaphragm/vanes (weak pump = low flow)
  • Check pump inlet blockage / moisture trap full
5) Check for line blockage / condensation:
  • Frozen/condensed lines restrict flow
  • Heat tracing failure causes condensation
  • Drain moisture traps and inspect tubing
6) After restoring flow:
  • Allow reading to stabilize
  • Verify with calibration gas
  • Confirm alarms clear and trend stable
