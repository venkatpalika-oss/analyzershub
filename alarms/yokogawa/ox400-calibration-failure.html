Alarm: Calibration Failure

Manufacturer: Yokogawa

Analyzer: OX400 Oxygen Analyzer

Alarm Description

Analyzer fails to complete zero or span calibration within specifications.

Typical Symptoms

  • Calibration cannot be completed
  • Span or zero readings out of limits
  • Repeated calibration errors after warm-up

Probable Causes

  1. Reference gas supply interrupted or incorrect
  2. Sensor or probe contaminated
  3. Heater not stable
  4. Signal wiring problem
  5. Electronics module failure

Field Checks

  • Verify reference gas flow and purity
  • Inspect sensor/probe for contamination
  • Check heater temperature stability
  • Check wiring and connections

Corrective Actions

  • Restore proper reference gas supply
  • Clean or replace sensor/probe
  • Ensure heater operates correctly
  • Repair wiring as needed
  • Replace electronics if calibration still fails
