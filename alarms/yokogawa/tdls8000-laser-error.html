Alarm: Laser Error

Manufacturer: Yokogawa

Analyzer: TDLS8000 Gas Analyzer

Alarm Description

Laser Error indicates that the TDLS laser emission is unstable or not detected. Accurate gas measurement is not possible until resolved.

Typical Symptoms

  • Analyzer shows zero or invalid gas readings
  • Repeated laser fault after power-on
  • Calibration fails or unstable

Probable Causes

  1. Laser diode failure
  2. Laser power supply problem
  3. Optical alignment misalignment
  4. Electronics module malfunction

Field Checks

  • Check laser power supply voltage
  • Inspect laser diode status LED
  • Check optical path and mirrors
  • Verify electronics module operation

Corrective Actions

  • Replace laser diode if defective
  • Repair or replace laser power supply
  • Realign optical components
  • Replace electronics module if necessary
