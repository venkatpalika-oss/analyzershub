Alarm: Heater Error

Manufacturer: Yokogawa

Analyzer: OX400 Oxygen Analyzer

Alarm Description

The Heater Error alarm indicates the sensor heater is not operating correctly. Accurate oxygen measurement is not possible unless the heater functions properly.

Typical Symptoms

  • Oxygen reading frozen, zero, or invalid
  • Analyzer warm-up state is prolonged
  • Repeated heater-related alarms after power-up

Probable Causes

  1. Heater element open or burned out
  2. Heater power supply failure
  3. Damaged or loose heater wiring
  4. Electronics module failure

Field Checks

  • Verify heater power and fuses
  • Check heater LED/status
  • Measure heater resistance
  • Inspect wiring and connectors

Corrective Actions

  • Replace heater if faulty
  • Restore heater power supply
  • Repair wiring or connectors
  • Replace electronics module if needed

Impact on Measurement

Oxygen measurement is unreliable. Do not use analyzer for process control or reporting until alarm cleared.
