Alarm: No Gas Signal

Manufacturer: Yokogawa

Analyzer: TDLS8000 Gas Analyzer

Alarm Description

No Gas Signal indicates the TDLS8000 is not detecting any valid gas concentration. Measurement cannot be trusted until resolved.

Typical Symptoms

  • Gas reading stuck at zero or baseline
  • Calibration cannot be performed
  • Laser and path appear normal

Probable Causes

  1. Obstruction in measurement path
  2. Gas not reaching the measurement location
  3. Laser power low or unstable
  4. Detector or electronics failure

Field Checks

  • Inspect measurement path for dust or blockage
  • Verify gas flow to measurement point
  • Check laser power and detector signals
  • Inspect electronics module

Corrective Actions

  • Clear measurement path of dust/debris
  • Ensure gas reaches analyzer
  • Repair/replace laser or detector if required
  • Replace electronics module if alarm persists
