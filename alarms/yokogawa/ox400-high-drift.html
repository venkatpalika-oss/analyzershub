Alarm: High Drift / Unstable Reading

Manufacturer: Yokogawa

Analyzer: OX400 Oxygen Analyzer

Alarm Description

Analyzer readings fluctuate beyond acceptable limits. Measurement is unstable.

Typical Symptoms

  • Oxygen readings oscillate rapidly
  • Analyzer fails to maintain span/zero
  • Calibration may fail intermittently

Probable Causes

  1. Sensor contamination or aging
  2. Reference air supply unstable
  3. Heater temperature fluctuations
  4. Signal wiring noise
  5. Electronics module instability

Field Checks

  • Inspect sensor/probe condition
  • Verify reference air flow stability
  • Check heater temperature
  • Inspect wiring for noise or loose connections

Corrective Actions

  • Clean or replace sensor/probe
  • Stabilize reference air supply
  • Ensure heater operates properly
  • Repair wiring and connections
  • Replace electronics if drift persists
