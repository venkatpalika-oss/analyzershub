Alarm: Calibration Failure

Manufacturer: Yokogawa

Analyzer: TDLS8000 Gas Analyzer

Alarm Description

Calibration Failure indicates the TDLS8000 cannot complete zero or span calibration. Measurement is unreliable until resolved.

Typical Symptoms

  • Zero or span calibration fails
  • Gas reading outside expected range
  • Repeated calibration errors after restart

Probable Causes

  1. Incorrect or unstable calibration gas
  2. Measurement path obstructed or contaminated
  3. Laser or detector misaligned
  4. Electronics module failure

Field Checks

  • Verify calibration gas type and flow
  • Inspect measurement path for contamination
  • Check laser output and detector alignment
  • Inspect electronics module

Corrective Actions

  • Replace or correct calibration gas supply
  • Clean or repair measurement path
  • Adjust or replace laser/detector if needed
  • Replace electronics module if alarm persists
