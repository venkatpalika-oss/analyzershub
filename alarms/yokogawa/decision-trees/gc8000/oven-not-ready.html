GC8000 — Oven Not Ready / Temperature Not Reached (Decision Tree)

Use when oven fails to reach setpoint or temp is unstable.

← Back to Yokogawa Trees GC8000 Main Page →

Decision Tree

1) Confirm setpoint and actual temperature
Is setpoint correct? Is actual temperature rising at all? Note any error codes.
2) Power supply and fuses
Check supply voltage, fuses/breakers for oven heater circuit.
3) Heater output switching?
Verify controller output/SSR/relay operation. No output → controller/SSR issue.
4) Temperature sensor fault
If reading is unrealistic or jumps → sensor/wiring fault.
5) Environmental/door/seal issues
If door open or insulation damaged → cannot hold temp. Check seals/insulation.
6) After repair
Allow oven to stabilize. Confirm retention times become stable before releasing to operation.

Why this matters

  • Oven temperature drift causes retention time shift
  • Retention shift causes wrong component identification and wrong results
  • Never calibrate while oven is unstable
