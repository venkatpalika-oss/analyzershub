OX400 High Drift / Unstable Reading - Decision Tree

Step-by-step diagnosis to resolve high drift alarms:

  1. Check sensor/probe for contamination or aging.
    • Good → Next step
    • Dirty or worn → Clean or replace probe
  2. Verify reference air stability.
    • Stable → Next step
    • Unstable → Fix air supply and filters
  3. Check heater temperature.
    • Stable → Next step
    • Fluctuating → Repair heater
  4. Inspect wiring for noise or loose connections.
    • OK → Next step
    • Faulty → Repair wiring
  5. Check electronics module.
    • Functional → Alarm should clear
    • Faulty → Replace electronics module
