Decision Tree – ZR22G Heater Error

  • Heater Error Alarm Active
    • Is analyzer powered ON?
      • No → Restore power supply
      • Yes → Check heater LED status
        • LED OFF → Measure heater resistance
          • Resistance open/short → Replace heater assembly
          • Resistance normal → Check heater power supply
        • LED ON → Check wiring between probe and converter
          • Wiring faulty → Repair wiring
          • Wiring OK → Replace electronics module

Field Tip: High flue temperature can also trigger heater protection.
