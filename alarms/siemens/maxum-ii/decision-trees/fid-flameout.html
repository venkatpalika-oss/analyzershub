Decision Tree: FID Flameout (If FID Installed)

Symptom: flame out alarm, ignition fails, unstable FID signal.

← Back to Trees ← Alarm Codes MAXUM II Main →
Safety: Follow site rules for hydrogen/fuel gas. Verify leak-free before ignition attempts.

Step-by-step isolation

1) Verify fuel gas and air supply pressures/flows are correct and stable (no regulator hunting).
2) Confirm ignition sequence: igniter working, wiring seated, controller not inhibiting ignition due to interlock.
3) Check for contamination:
  • Dirty jet/nozzle causes unstable flame.
  • Moisture or oil in gas lines can cause flameout.
4) Inspect for leaks and venting: ensure exhaust path is clear and properly routed.
5) Validate with stable standard run: if flame holds but signal unstable → check electrometer/grounding.
6) Corrective actions:
  • Stabilize fuel/air regulators; replace clogged filters.
  • Clean/replace FID jet as per manual.
  • Verify detector electronics and grounding.
