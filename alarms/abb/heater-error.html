Alarm: Heater Error

Manufacturer: ABB
Applicable Analyzers: AZ10, AZ20, AZ30

Oxygen measurement is unreliable when the sensor heater is not operating correctly.

Alarm Description

This alarm indicates that the zirconia sensor heater is not maintaining the required operating temperature.

Probable Causes

  • Heater element open circuit or burnout
  • Heater power supply or fuse failure
  • Loose or damaged heater wiring
  • Excessive process temperature
  • Heater control electronics fault

Field Checks

  • Verify heater status and warm-up indication
  • Measure heater resistance
  • Check heater voltage at probe terminals
  • Inspect wiring and connectors
  • Confirm process temperature is within limits

Corrective Actions

  • Replace heater or probe assembly
  • Restore heater power supply
  • Repair wiring or connectors
  • Relocate probe or install heat shielding
