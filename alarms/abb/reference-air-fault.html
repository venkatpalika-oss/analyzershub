Alarm: Reference Air / Purge Fault

Reference air or purge supply is critical for accurate analyzer operation.

Probable Causes

  • Reference air filter blocked
  • Loss of reference air supply
  • Purge solenoid valve failure
  • Leaks or broken tubing

Corrective Actions

  • Restore reference air supply
  • Replace filters
  • Repair tubing or valves
