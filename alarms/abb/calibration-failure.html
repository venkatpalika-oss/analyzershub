Calibration Fault — ABB AZ10

This alarm indicates that the analyzer failed to complete a zero or span calibration within acceptable limits.

Probable Causes

  • Calibration gas not reaching the sensor
  • Incorrect, expired, or contaminated calibration gas
  • Calibration solenoid valve not operating
  • Leaks or blockage in calibration tubing
  • Slow or degraded sensor response

Corrective Actions

  • Verify calibration gas pressure and flow rate
  • Confirm correct gas concentration and cylinder validity
  • Check calibration valve actuation and piping
  • Leak-test calibration lines and fittings
  • Replace sensor if response time is excessive
Technician note: Calibration failures are often secondary to reference air, heater, or sampling issues. Verify those first before replacing sensors.
