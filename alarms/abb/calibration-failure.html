Alarm: Calibration Failure

Applicable Analyzers: AZ10, AZ20, AZ25, AZ30, AZ40

Probable Causes

  • Calibration gas not reaching the sensor
  • Incorrect or expired calibration gas
  • Calibration solenoid valve failure
  • Leaks or blockage in calibration tubing
  • Slow or degraded sensor response

Corrective Actions

  • Verify calibration gas pressure and flow
  • Check calibration valve operation
  • Replace tubing or fittings
  • Replace sensor if response is slow
