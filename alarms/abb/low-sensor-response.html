Alarm: Low Sensor Response

Common in zirconia and TDLAS analyzers when signal strength is reduced.

Probable Causes

  • Sensor aging or contamination
  • Optical window fouling (TDLAS)
  • Low process gas flow
  • Sample path blockage
  • Incorrect installation

Corrective Actions

  • Clean or replace sensor / optics
  • Verify probe alignment
  • Restore process gas flow
