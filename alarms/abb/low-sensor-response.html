Low Sensor Response — ABB AZ10

This alarm indicates that the zirconia oxygen sensor response is weak, slow, or outside expected limits during operation or calibration.

Probable Causes

  • Sensor aging or loss of sensitivity
  • Sensor contamination from dust, sulfur, or corrosives
  • Low or unstable process gas flow at the probe
  • Partial blockage of the sensor tip or diffusion path
  • Incorrect probe installation or mounting angle

Field Checks

  • Verify process gas flow and velocity at the probe
  • Inspect sensor tip for fouling or deposits
  • Confirm probe orientation and insertion depth
  • Check reference air flow and heater status
  • Perform a response test using calibration gas

Corrective Actions

  • Clean or replace the zirconia sensor
  • Restore proper process gas flow conditions
  • Correct probe installation or alignment
  • Replace sensor if response remains slow or unstable
Technician note: Low sensor response alarms are often secondary effects of poor process conditions or contamination. Always address the root cause before replacing sensors.
