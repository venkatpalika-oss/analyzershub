Rosemount 6888 High Drift / Unstable Reading - Decision Tree

Step-by-step troubleshooting for high drift alarms:

  1. Inspect sensor and heater
    • Good → Next step
    • Dirty/worn → Clean or replace sensor/heater
  2. Check electronics module
    • Functional → Next step
    • Faulty → Replace module
  3. Verify wiring/connectors
    • Secure → Next step
    • Loose/damaged → Repair wiring/connectors
  4. Inspect measurement environment for disturbances
    • Stable → Alarm cleared
    • Unstable → Stabilize environment
