Alarm: Calibration Failure

Manufacturer: Rosemount

Analyzer: 6888 Oxygen Analyzer

Alarm Description

Calibration Failure indicates that the analyzer cannot complete zero or span calibration. Measurement may be inaccurate until resolved.

Typical Symptoms

  • Zero/span calibration fails repeatedly
  • Oxygen readings outside expected range
  • Heater and sensor appear normal

Probable Causes

  1. Incorrect calibration gas or flow
  2. Obstructed or contaminated sensor
  3. Electronics module issue
  4. Wiring or connection problem

Field Checks

  • Verify calibration gas type and pressure
  • Inspect sensor for contamination or damage
  • Check electronics module functionality
  • Check wiring and connections

Corrective Actions

  • Replace or adjust calibration gas supply
  • Clean or replace sensor if needed
  • Repair or replace electronics module
  • Fix wiring/connectors
