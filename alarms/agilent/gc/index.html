How to use this alarm hub

  1. Identify the alarm or symptom shown by the GC
  2. Confirm utilities, carrier gas, and sample system first
  3. Follow checks in the listed order
  4. Verify recovery with stable chromatograms
Field rule: GC alarms are most often caused by sample system issues, carrier instability, or valve problems — not electronics.
