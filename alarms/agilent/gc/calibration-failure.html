Calibration Failure

GC fails to meet expected response during calibration.

Common Causes

  1. Incorrect or expired calibration gas
  2. Unstable baseline or detector fault
  3. Injection repeatability issues
  4. Leaks or restrictions in the sample path

Corrective Actions

  • Verify calibration gas concentration and expiry date
  • Allow GC to stabilize fully before calibration
  • Leak-check injection, columns, and fittings
  • Repeat calibration after corrective actions
