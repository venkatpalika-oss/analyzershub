Calibration Failure

GC fails to meet expected response during calibration.

Common Causes

  1. Incorrect or expired calibration gas
  2. Unstable baseline or detector fault
  3. Injection repeatability issues
  4. Leaks or restrictions

Corrective Actions

  • Verify calibration gas concentration and expiry
  • Stabilize system before calibration
  • Repair leaks and confirm injection stability
  • Repeat calibration after corrections
