FID – No Response

No peaks or zero signal from the FID detector.

Probable Causes (Field Order)

  • FID flame not ignited
  • Hydrogen or air supply missing
  • Jet blocked or contaminated
  • Electrometer cable disconnected
  • Detector electronics fault

Field Checks

  • Confirm flame status in GC diagnostics
  • Verify H₂ / air pressure and flow
  • Inspect and clean FID jet
  • Check signal cable continuity
  • Restart detector after correction
Technician rule: Never recalibrate an FID with no flame.
