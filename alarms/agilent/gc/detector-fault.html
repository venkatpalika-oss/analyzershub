Detector Fault

Detector reports error, unstable baseline, or no response.

Typical Causes

  • Detector power loss
  • Contamination or damage
  • Electronics failure
  • Incorrect configuration

Corrective Actions

  • Restore detector power
  • Clean or replace detector components
  • Verify configuration and firmware
  • Replace detector module if required
