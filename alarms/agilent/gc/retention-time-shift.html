Retention Time Shift

Peaks appear earlier or later than expected despite stable process conditions.

← Agilent GC Alarms Agilent GC

What this condition means

Retention time shift indicates a change in carrier gas flow, oven temperature, or column characteristics.

Most Common Causes

  1. Carrier gas flow or pressure change
  2. Oven temperature drift or control issue
  3. Column contamination or aging
  4. Restriction or partial blockage
  5. Incorrect method parameters

Field Checks

  • Verify carrier gas pressure and flow controllers
  • Check oven temperature trend vs setpoint
  • Inspect column for contamination or damage
  • Review recent method or hardware changes

Corrective Actions

  • Restore correct carrier gas conditions
  • Recalibrate oven temperature if required
  • Condition or replace contaminated columns
  • Correct method configuration
