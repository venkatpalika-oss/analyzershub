TCD – Low Sensitivity

Peak areas lower than expected or slowly degrading.

Common Causes

  • Contaminated filament
  • Carrier gas purity issue
  • Flow imbalance across detector
  • Column or restriction problem

Corrective Actions

  • Verify carrier gas type and purity
  • Balance reference and sample flow
  • Check restrictions and leaks
  • Replace filament if degraded
