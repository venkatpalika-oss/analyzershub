Calibration Fault – ABB AZ Series

  • Step 1: Check zero gas calibration. Is the reading stable?
  • If No: Inspect zero gas cylinder pressure, regulator, and connections.
  • If Yes: Proceed to span calibration.
  • Step 2: Check span gas calibration. Is reading within tolerance?
  • If No: Replace span gas cylinder or clean gas lines.
  • If Yes: Check sensor drift trends and history in diagnostics menu.
  • Step 3: If persistent error, schedule sensor replacement or full calibration procedure.
