ZR22G – Stable but Incorrect Oxygen Reading

Analyzer: ZR22G · Topic: Sample System · Contributor: Field Technician

Observed Symptoms

  • Stable oxygen reading lower than expected
  • No analyzer alarms
  • Calibration passed

Root Cause

Condensation formed in the sample line during low load operation, partially blocking sample flow.

Resolution

  • Improved sample line heating
  • Re-routed low points
  • Verified probe temperature
Lesson: Stable readings do not always mean valid measurements.
